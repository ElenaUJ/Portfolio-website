Hi there! I'm Elena, a former biologist who found her true passion in web development.

In my previous life as a scientist, I confidently navigated through the challenges of molecular biology, bioinformatics, and data analysis. This experience sharpened my analytical and critical thinking skills, and helped me grow both personally and professionally. However, my journey took an exciting turn when I fell in love with coding...

My unique blend of strengths—organization, systematic thinking, and love for problem-solving—provides me with a strong foundation to be an efficient and fast-learning developer. Now, armed with a growing tech skillset including HTML, CSS, and the MERN stack amongst others, I'm on a mission to put those skills to a meaningful use.

I'm on the lookout for exciting opportunities to contribute innovative solutions to impactful, and sustainable, projects in Toronto (CA), and all over the world.
